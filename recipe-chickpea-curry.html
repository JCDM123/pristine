Chickpea & Green Bean Curry

Prep10 min
Cook25 min
Total35 min
Serves4
DifficultyEasy
Share
Adjust servings
4 servings
4
Metric
Imperial
Main ingredients
400gchickpeas, drained
200ggreen beans, trimmed
400mlcoconut milk
400gcrushed tomatoes
1large onion, diced
4garlic cloves, minced
Spices
2 tspground turmeric
2 tspground cumin
1 tspgaram masala
1 tspground coriander
To serve
2 cupsbasmati rice, cooked
1lime, cut into wedges
1 handfulfresh coriander

A deeply nourishing, plant-based curry that celebrates the anti-inflammatory power of turmeric and the wholesome goodness of chickpeas, a staple in our family kitchen.

Chef's tip

Toast your dry spices in the pan for 30 seconds before adding the onion. The heat releases the essential oils and transforms the dish entirely.

Method

1
Toast the spices

Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add turmeric, cumin, garam masala and coriander directly to the dry pan. Toast for 30 seconds, stirring constantly, until fragrant.

Start 30s timer
2
Sauté the aromatics

Add olive oil or coconut oil. Add diced onion and cook for 5–6 minutes until soft. Add minced garlic and cook for a further 2 minutes.

Start 8 min timer
3
Build the sauce

Add crushed tomatoes and stir well. Cook for 3–4 minutes until tomatoes deepen in colour and the oil begins to separate around the edges.

Start 4 min timer
4
Add chickpeas & coconut milk

Add drained chickpeas and coconut milk. Stir to combine and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Start 10 min timer
5
Add green beans & finish

Add trimmed green beans and cook for a final 5 minutes until just tender but still vibrant green. Taste and season. Serve over basmati rice with lime and fresh coriander.

Start 5 min timer
