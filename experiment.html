The Experiment

Two jars. Two waters. One camera.
No edits. No filters. No interference.

We placed the same berries in two jars — one filled with MEA structured water, one with filtered tap water. Both jars sit in the same light. Both are photographed with the same camera. We document what happens and share it here, exactly as it is.

The setup

Same berries. Same conditions. 18 months apart.

The Pristine Wellness logo sits on both jars so you always know what you're looking at. Both jars live in the same spot, under the same light, photographed at the same time every update.

The only variable is the water. One jar contains MEA structured water — developed by water quality scientist Robert Gourlay after 20 years of research. The other contains filtered tap water.

We don't touch them. We don't edit the photos. We just document what happens.
