The Experiment

Two jars. Two waters. One camera.

Both jars were filled on 25 November 2024 with the same berries and the same amount of water. One contains filtered tap water. The other contains MEA structured water developed by water quality scientist Robert Gourlay. Photographed weekly, unedited.

Where it started

Filled 25 November 2024. Still running.

This is the original experiment. Both jars have been sitting undisturbed since November 2024. The photo alongside was taken 18 months later. Nothing was added, nothing was changed. The only difference is the water.

The tap water jar is now black and opaque. The MEA water jar remains clear with the berries still intact.

Robert Gourlay's research shows that MEA structured water maintains cellular frequency in organic matter. This experiment documents that claim in real time with no interference.
